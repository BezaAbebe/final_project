Data Cleaning Process

We began cleaning this large dataset by applying various functions to recognize and address incomplete, incorrect, inaccurate, or irrelevant data. This process involved:

  • Modifying: Correcting errors in the data.
  • Replacing: Substituting missing or incorrect data with appropriate values.
  • Deleting: Removing rows or columns that were irrelevant.
Finalizing the Cleaned Data
